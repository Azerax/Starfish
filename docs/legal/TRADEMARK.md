# "Governed by Starfish" Mark — Usage Terms (DRAFT — pending counsel review)

> **DRAFT.** This document is a starting point and has **not** been reviewed by legal counsel. Do not treat
> it as final terms. Counsel review is required before any external/design-partner launch.

The "Governed by Starfish" mark indicates that a product runs the Starfish governance engine in a
deny-by-default, fail-closed configuration. Intended (draft) conditions of use:

- The mark may be used only while Starfish governance is actually enforcing (not disabled/bypassed).
- The mark must not imply Anthropic or third-party endorsement, certification, or warranty.
- The mark must not be altered; use the provided wordmark/lockup.
- Misrepresenting ungoverned software as "Governed by Starfish" is prohibited.

Open items for counsel: ownership/registration of the mark, licensing scope, revocation, and geography.
