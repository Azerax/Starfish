## No warranty (draft)
Starfish is provided "AS IS", without warranty of any kind. Starfish reduces the blast radius of agent
actions via deny-by-default governance; it does **not** guarantee that a governed agent is safe, correct, or
compliant. The adopter remains responsible for their agents, policies, keys, and operations.

## Scope statement (draft)
Starfish governs tool calls, egress, budgets, and approvals at the boundaries it is wired into. It cannot
govern actions taken outside those boundaries (e.g., a host process that bypasses the PEP). Correct
integration (all side-effectful paths routed through the governed executor/sidecar) is the adopter's
responsibility.
